Welcome

Welcome to demonline, the UCT Physics Department website for educational demonstrations. The website catalogues both lecture demonstrations and VPython scripts. The lecture demos section contains descriptions of the setup and execution of various physics experiments, along with screenshots and a reference for finding the equipment in the UCT Physics labs. The VPython scripts section contains scripts that demonstrate various physics concepts.
